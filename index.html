Tarneit P-9 College

ABOUT US

Established in 2013.

Tarneit-P-9-College was established in 2013 as a new school. Our school enrolls any child that is in the years Prep to Year 9. Each year, we have a STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths) event held at our school which happens after school.

Gymnasium and Performing Arts/Music Room

Bulit in late 2013-early 2014.

Our gym and Performing Arts room are built in the same buidling. The gym is either used for different lessons of P.E. or it is used for events such as assemblies, graduations and different announcements. On the other hand, the Performing Arts room is used for classes of drama or dance, depending on the year level.

Playgrounds

Bulit in late 2014.

These playgrounds are built to entertain students during recess and lunch hours. Across the whole school, there are approximately 4, one being for preps, one for Year 1s, and one for Year 2s and above.

School Acheivements

Interschool Cross Country Race (Won 2nd place)
Won 2nd place in a "Cross Country" race.

In a "Cross Country" race, a group of students are chosen from each school in Victoria, Australia to participate in a relay race among other schools. In this event, the students representing our school won the 2nd place prize.

Interschool Cricket Competition
Winning team in an inter school cricket tournament.

Every semester, our school's physical education choose students to participate in this tournament. In the previous tournament we played, the group of students representing Tarneit P-9 College were the winning team, against 6 other teams.
